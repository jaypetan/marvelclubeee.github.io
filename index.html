MARVEL

A game and innovation development club @ NTU EEE

ABOUT

Marvel Club@EEE is a place that where members can gather and create marvellous, fun and exciting stuff, like computer or video games, VR/AR games, board games and even theme park simulator rides, and possibly showcasing their creations in competitions like Independent Games Festival! Besides these, members can also develop innovative technological solutions to solve world issues. In short, to create MARVEL!

EVENTS

Playtesting Session

Club members gathered to test our exciting games and meet the talented competition teams behind them. We were honored to host an alumni, Duan Kai, who is a game developer at Junkfish and provided valuable feedback on our games and gave us an insight into game design philosophy.

Blender Workshop

The aim of this workshop is to teach EEE/IEM students the basics of how to design, model, texture and animate 3D assets for a game. Target audience consists of game competition team members and other interested students.

Gamescom Outing

A club initiative to allow fellow club members to experience the world of video games and make connections with fellow game developers.

EEE Open House

Promoted our club and showcased games made by competition teams to attract new members.

Enitio Sharing

Signals the birth of MARVEL club as we introduced ourselves to the freshmen for the very first time during orientation.

PROJECTS

Earthlings

Earthlings is a two-player 2.5D environment game that promotes ecological awareness by encouraging cooperation in the game. By developing this game, we hope to prompt our players to think about the relationship between humans, other creatures, and nature.

Download game

Deja Vu

A 2D puzzle platformer that utilizes a variety of elemental potions to overcome environmental obstacles and hazards.

Download game

Bee Game

A 3D game that simulates the life of a bee. The game aims to educate players about the life of a bee and the importance of bees in the ecosystem.
